Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Booking an appointment is easy! Simply click the "Book Now" button, select your desired service, choose a staff member (optional), pick your preferred date and time, and fill in your contact details. You'll receive an instant confirmation via email.

Yes! You can reschedule or cancel your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Simply log into your account, go to "My Appointments," and click the reschedule or cancel button. Please note that cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may incur a fee.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment can be made online during booking or in person at the time of your appointment.

Yes! We offer remote consultations via video call for most of our services. When booking, simply select "Remote Session" as your preference. You'll receive a video call link via email before your appointment.

You'll receive automatic reminders via email and SMS (if you've provided your phone number) 24 hours before your appointment and again 2 hours before. You can manage your notification preferences in your account settings.

If you cancel your appointment more than 24 hours in advance, you'll receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the appointment are subject to a 50% cancellation fee. No-shows are non-refundable. All refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
